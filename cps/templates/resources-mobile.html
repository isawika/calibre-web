{{_('Reading the Storybooks on a Mobile Device')}}

{{_('Android')}}

  1. Open the Play Store app, and search for "pocketbook reader".
  2. Click the "Install" button.
  3. Open the PocketBook app.
  4. When it asks for permission to "access photos, media and files on your device", choose Allow.
    • If you already have ebooks in your mobile device, the app will display them in its Home page.

    Now, let's make the Philippine Storybooks library available on the app.

  5. Click on the menu icon (on the upper left),
    then select *Network Libraries* from the dropdown list.
  6. Click the folder icon with the '+' symbol.
  7. Enter these values in the dialog box, then click 'OK'.
    • URL https://philippinestorybooks.org/opds
    • Title Philippine Storybooks


  8. You should now see Philippine Storybooks listed. Go ahead and select it.

  9. You can search the Philippine Storybooks by title, Author, Language, etc. by clicking the item.

    Here for example is a sample of the titles. Select any book to see more details.


  10. Some books are available on both EPUB and PDF formats, while others are only available in one format. (We will make more books available as EPUB in the future.)

Tips

  • To avoid mobile data charges, download the books when you are connected to WiFi.
  • To view your downloaded books, go to the main menu and select "All books". If needed, you can filter by type such as "EPUB"
